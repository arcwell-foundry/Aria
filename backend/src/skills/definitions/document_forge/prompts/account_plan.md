## Instructions

Produce a structured account plan with the following sections:

1. **Executive Summary** — One-paragraph overview of the account opportunity, current relationship status, and strategic importance.

2. **Account Overview** — Company profile, industry segment, size, recent news, and strategic priorities relevant to our value proposition.

3. **Stakeholder Map** — Key decision-makers and influencers with their roles, priorities, and relationship strength. Identify champions, blockers, and unknowns.

4. **Opportunity Assessment** — Current pipeline, deal stages, estimated revenue potential, and probability-weighted forecast.

5. **Competitive Landscape** — Known competitors in the account, their positioning, and our differentiation points.

6. **Engagement Strategy** — Recommended approach for the next 90 days including specific actions, stakeholder-specific messaging, and milestones.

7. **Risk Factors** — Identified risks to the account relationship or deal progression, with mitigation strategies.

8. **Success Metrics** — Measurable KPIs to track account health and plan effectiveness.

Include the template name "account_plan" and list all context sources used in the metadata.
